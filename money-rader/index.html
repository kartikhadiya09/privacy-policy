Money Radar respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have when using the Money Radar mobile application (the “App”).

1. Who We Are

  • App Name: Money Radar
  • Developer: Kartikkumar Hadiya
  • Contact Email: mr.kartikhadiya1617@gmail.com

2. Data We Collect

We do not collect or store your personal or financial data on our servers.

The App stores the following only on your device:

  • Transactions (income, expense, transfers)

  • App settings

3. Google Drive Backup (Optional)

  • The backup file is saved to your own Google Drive account.
  • We do not receive or access your backup file.
  • You can disable or skip backup at any time.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your data with third parties.

The only exception is when you choose to connect your own Google account for backup. In that case, data is stored in your Google Drive under your control.

5. Data Security

  • Data stays on your device.
  • No central server storage.
  • Optional backups are stored in your Google Drive.

Because data is stored locally, please secure your device and Google account.

6. Children’s Privacy

Money Radar can be used by all age groups. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children.

7. Your Choices

  • Use the App without providing any personal information.
  • Enable or disable Google Drive backup anytime.
  • Delete your data by uninstalling the App or clearing app storage.

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted within the App or on our official channels.
